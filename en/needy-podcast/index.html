About the Podcast

Why Needy?

Conflicts are a normal part of life. Thankfully, we're all unique – which makes life interesting, and conversations complicated.

Needy gives you inspiration on how to use conflict to build a deeper connection: to what's alive in you, and in others. You'll learn about different communication approaches, with founding principles rooted in Marshall Rosenberg's Nonviolent Communication – alongside applied insights from improvisation theatre.

The name is intentional. We all have needs. The question is what we do with them.
