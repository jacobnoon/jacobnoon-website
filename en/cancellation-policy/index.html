Cancellation Policy

As of May 2026. This policy applies to all booked trainings, coaching sessions and mediation assignments with Jacob Noon – Noon Coaching.

Scope

The following terms apply to individuals and organisations that have booked services from Jacob Noon – Noon Coaching (hereinafter "Provider"). "Fee" refers to the total fee agreed in the offer or contract for the respective appointment or format.

Cancellation Periods and Fees

Cancellations are only valid in text form (email to mail@jacobnoon.com). The decisive date is the time of receipt of the cancellation notice.

Time of Cancellation Cancellation Fee
More than 30 days before the appointment No charge
15 to 30 days before the appointment 20% of the agreed fee
8 to 14 days before the appointment 50% of the agreed fee
7 days or fewer before the appointment 100% of the agreed fee

Rescheduling

One free rescheduling is possible up to 14 days before the agreed date, provided a new date is agreed within six months. Further reschedules or changes made within the 14-day window will be treated as cancellations according to the table above.

Force Majeure and Exceptional Circumstances

In cases of documented exceptional circumstances (e.g. sudden serious illness, accident or government-mandated measures) that make attendance impossible, cancellation fees will be reviewed individually on a goodwill basis. In such cases, please contact us as early as possible and provide appropriate documentation.

Cancellation by the Provider

Should an appointment need to be cancelled by the Provider (e.g. illness, emergency), any payments already made will be refunded in full or — by agreement — credited to a replacement date. Further claims are excluded unless there is gross negligence or wilful misconduct on the part of the Provider.
