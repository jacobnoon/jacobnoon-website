My approach

Experience before theory. Always.

Every unit starts with a situation, a dilemma, a provocation. The cognitive framework comes after the experience – not before it. That's not a didactic trick. It's why the learning sticks.

I come from improvisational theatre. That means I can work with what's actually happening in the room – not just what's on the schedule.
