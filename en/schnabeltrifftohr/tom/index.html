Self-revelation Beak

Tom, the I-Narrator

Tom (or Tabea) sends I-messages and takes a very active part in the conversation by sharing his view of the world. Open and present – sometimes a lot of air-time.

"I have a feeling I … need."

Play recommendations

  • Speak consistently from the I-perspective.
  • Share thoughts, feelings and inner processes in detail.
  • Justify your behaviour with your inner state.
  • Tell back-stories and context.
  • Steer the conversation back to yourself.
👉 Rule of thumb: Self-revelation before content and relationship.
Risk

Verbal flood, self-centredness, performance – self-revelation tips into self-presentation.

Body language

  • Lively, active body language and voice.
  • Lots of gestures, often explanatory or embellishing.
  • Strong eye contact, looking for resonance.
  • Lots of variation between closeness and distance.
  • Visible emotional involvement with a strong self-reference.

Signature lines

  • "I'm experiencing this as…"
  • "For me this feels like…"
  • "I noticed that I…"
  • "Let me briefly explain what's going on with me…"
  • "That really triggers me…"
